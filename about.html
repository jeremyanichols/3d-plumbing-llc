For almost 20 years, people in Manchester and across St. Louis County have called 3-D Plumbing when they want the job done right. Here's who we are.

Our Story

Run by the Demuri Family Since 2005

3-D Plumbing, LLC is a family-owned union shop based in Manchester. We started in 2005, but our plumbing experience goes back more than 35 years. The people who answer your call are the same people who stand behind the work.

We've built our name the way good tradespeople do: by showing up, charging a fair price, and fixing the real problem. A lot of our work comes from repeat customers and neighbors who pass our name along, and that's how we like it.

From a water heater swap to a tenant build-out, you get a licensed plumber who knows the code and respects your home.
